!1 RecordRelation tests
The goal of this test is to show that it is possible to create a recordRelation.

A record relation, contains of two parts,

a refRecordLinkId pointing to metadata describing a record link that connects one record to another record and

a refRecordGroupId pointing to metadata describing a group that contains information about the nature of the link.
